# Cleanfinity Filter

## Overview

If a basic basket with central filter for the MELAtherm 10 and MELAtherm 10 Evolution (Order number 80440) is available, the white plastic central filter can be directly replaced with the Cleanfinity filter. Cleaning of the permanent central filter is carried out using the supplied cleaning brush under running water.

**Note:** The Cleanfinity filter is not suitable for ophthalmology.
