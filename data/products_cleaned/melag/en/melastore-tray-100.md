# MELAstore Tray 100

## Overview

Specially developed for the MELAtherm® 10 and MELAtherm® 20 Series, the MELAstore® Tray 100 (dimensions W x H x D: 17.9 x 3 x 28 cm) made of high-quality stainless steel ensures excellent cleaning results due to its large openings and enables quick and safe handling of individual instruments and instrument sets (e.g., for periodontal treatments, conservative treatments or minor surgical procedures).

## Benefits

- Significant time saving
- Increased processing quality through standardisation
- Effective damage prevention
- Improved safety for the practice team
- Variable silicone supports ensure stable and secure fixation of the instruments
- Ability to label each tray with an individual identification label supports seamless documentation of the reprocessing procedures
