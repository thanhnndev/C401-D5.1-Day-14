# Basis Basket with Injector Rail

## Overview

The basis basket with injector rail is fitted with 11 injector nozzles and clamp springs and can be equipped with various adapters for hollow instruments as required. It provides space for insert racks and baskets in 6 flex segments in up to 2 levels.
