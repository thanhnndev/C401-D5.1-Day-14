# MELAstore Transport Box

## Overview

The MELAstore transport box offers sufficient space for individual instruments and up to 5 MELAstore Trays 100 or 4 MELAstore Trays 200, thus ensuring the safe transportation of contaminated instruments from the treatment room to the reprocessing room. This solution protects practice staff and patients from contamination and puncture injuries while complying with hygiene regulations.
