# Vacuclave 105

## Overview

Big on the inside, small on the outside, special all round.

With the fastest autoclave of their class, we are redefining the limits of speed: with the Vacuclave 105, you can sterilise your instruments on 2 trays in record time from 6.5 minutes (plus drying) - and in the smallest of spaces.

The powerhouse with its 5-litre chamber is a compact class B autoclave that achieves great things: Ideal as an addition to your practice autoclave for quick sterilisations between treatments or as a stand-alone device for smaller quantities of instruments.
