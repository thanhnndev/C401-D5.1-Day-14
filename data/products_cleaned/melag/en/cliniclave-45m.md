# Cliniclave 45 M

## Overview

The M stands for Magnum: The large 200-liter autoclave sterilizes up to two STE or 70 kg load in record time.

## Features

- Large 200-liter chamber capacity
- Sterilizes up to two STE (Sterilization Units)
- 70 kg load capacity
- Record-time sterilization cycles
