# Basis Basket without Injector Rail

## Overview

The basic basket for MELAtherm 10 and MELAtherm 10 Evolution without injection rail is suitable for practices that do not need to process hollow body instruments. It provides space for insert racks and baskets across 6 Flex segments in up to 2 levels.
