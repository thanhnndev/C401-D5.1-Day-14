# MELAstore Tray Ophthalmology

## Overview

Optimize the instrument reprocessing in your ophthalmological practice or clinic with the MELAstore Tray Ophthalmology. Specifically designed for the demanding requirements of ophthalmology, the MELAstore Tray ensures maximum efficiency and safety throughout the reprocessing of your cataract surgery sets.

## Features

**Flexible and Secure**
The MELAstore Tray consists of an upper and a lower basket, equipped with flexible, positionable silicone supports. These guarantee a secure hold for both rigid and articulated instruments.

**Effective Cleaning**
The lower basket features an injection rail and versatile connections to ensure reliable cleaning and disinfection of your complex hollow instruments (e.g., phaco, irrigation, and suction handpieces).

**Time-Saving**
Instruments are inserted directly into the silicone supports after the procedure, significantly speeding up preparation and reprocessing through a standardised process.

**Space-Saving**
Up to 6 or 8 MELAstore Trays Ophthalmology can be space-efficiently placed in the universal holder of the MELAtherm 10 or MELAtherm 20 and optimally adapted for cleaning and disinfection.

**Protection Against Recontamination**
After cleaning and disinfection, the upper and lower baskets are combined into a set and packed in a MELAstore Box 200. The integrated filter protects the instrument set from recontamination during storage.

## Compatibility

The MELAstore Tray Ophthalmology is only compatible with the MELAtherm 10 base basket with injection rail and plastic central filter (Art.-Nr. ME80440) or the Cleanfinity Pro (Art.-Nr. ME84650) central filter of the MELAtherm 20.
