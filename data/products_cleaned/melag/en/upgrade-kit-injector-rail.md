# Upgrade Kit Injector Rail

## Overview

The upgrade kit enables the cleanable central filter to be accommodated, which filters all 11 connections simultaneously. The Cleanfinity Filter is therefore particularly economical for the reprocessing of hollow-body instruments with an internal diameter of 0.8 mm, such as transmission instruments, powder jet heads and ultrasonic tips.

The integrated control indicator in the injector rail shows when the filter is clogged and should be cleaned. The central filter is then cleaned under running water with the cleaning brush supplied in the scope of delivery.

## Compatibility

The upgrade kit is suitable for the basic baskets with injector rail (order no. 00197 and 00200). The existing adapters can be used without any problems. The basic basket without injector rail (order no. 00188) cannot be converted.
