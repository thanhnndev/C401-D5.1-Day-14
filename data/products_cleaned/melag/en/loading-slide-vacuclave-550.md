# Loading Slide for Vacuclave 550

## Overview

The loading drawer for up to 10 MELAstore boxes enables convenient loading and unloading of the autoclave with sterilization containers. 

## Compatibility

For the reprocessing of trays and individual instruments it is required to combine the loading drawer with the mount Comfort. (Not compatible with mount Basic).
