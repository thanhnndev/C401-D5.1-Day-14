# MELAstore Box 100

## Overview

MELAstore enables an efficient workflow from patient treatment to reprocessing and storage of instruments. The use of the MELAstore Box 100 saves a lot of time, consumables and money every day compared to sealing and packaging individual instruments.

After cleaning and disinfection, the MELAstore Trays are placed directly in the MELAstore Box and sealed - this already completes the packaging process. This is because the filter inserted in the lid ensures the sterilization results in the long term. The filter is available as a disposable paper filter or as a permanent filter.

For labeling the MELAstore Box, MELAG offers labeling plates with and without barcode inscription to ensure complete documentation.

## Specifications

- Dimensions (W x H x D): 19 x 4.6 x 31.2 cm
- For holding two MELAstore Trays 50 or one MELAstore Tray 100
