# Cliniclave 45

## Overview

Compact, fast, economical. The large autoclave with 105 liters for reprocessing a sterilization unit in the shortest possible time.

## Cliniclave 45 Series

You decide: Extra large or extra extra large.

Sterilizing up to 70 kg of instruments in just 23 - 50 minutes - is that possible? Yes, with Cliniclave 45. With 4 models, the Cliniclave Series offers the greatest possible flexibility, outstanding performance and smart functions. Discover the unique XXL device concept available as a single or double door unit with a chamber size of 100 - 200 litres.

## Benefits

### Large Chamber. Huge Benefits.

The Cliniclave Series has been designed for large loads down to the smallest detail. The sterilization of large quantities of instruments and textiles and their quick availability is one of the most important requirements in clinics, large practices and outpatient centers.

With a load capacity of up to 70 kg of instruments or 14 kg of textiles, the Cliniclave 45 Series provides far greater performance than almost all other large steam sterilizers.

### Extra-Large Display. For Extra-Large Operating Comfort.

The incredibly advanced Smart-Touch Display enables intuitive operation, helps to avoid errors and to find all important information quickly.

The integrated ProControl approval software allows complete documentation. For secure traceability, an individual PIN can be assigned for all users. Authentication for batch approval is thus carried out quickly and securely on the display.

### Quick-Fact: Sterilisation in Record Time

With the Double Jacket Technology, you sterilize at double speed in only 13 min. with the Quick Program.
